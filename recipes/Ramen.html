Ramen

Description

Ramen is a college staple and with good reason. This meal is CHEAP, delicious, and incredibly easy to make. For this recipe, I will be using chicken flavor (the best one). If you want to spice things up, add an egg at the end for a cheap source of protein. Only a pot and a source of water are needed to make this meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 Chicken Ramen Pack
  • 2 Cups of water

Steps

  1. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil.
  2. Once water is boiling, add Ramen and stir for 2-3 minutes making sure to break up noodles.
  3. Drain water from pot.
  4. Add flavor packet and mix. I personally use half of the flavor packet as it tastes just as good but cuts sodium and fats in half.
