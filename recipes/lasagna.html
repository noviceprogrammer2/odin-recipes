Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for the popular dish Lasagna! Enjoyed by cats and people alike, with the right amount of work it will turn out great. For this recipe, it will take approximately 30 minutes to prep, 2hrs and 30 minutes to cook, and an additional 15 minutes. This recipe can serve 12 people too! For this recipe we will need a dutch oven to mix and cook ingredients for a few hours. We will also need a large pot, and and an oven. Some of the nutrional facts include the following: 448 calories, 29.7 grams of protein, 36.5 grams of carbohydrates, and 21.3 grams of fat. If able too, I suggest serving this delicious meal with some garlic bread!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6ounce) cans of tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In a Dutch Oven, cook ground beef, sausage, onlin, and garlic on medium heat until browned. Then, stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water. Season with sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, 1 tsp salt, pepper, and 2 tablespoons of parsley. Let simmer, covered, for 1 1/2 hours while stirring occasionally.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Then, cook lasagna noodles in the pot for 8 to 10 minutes. Once cooked, drain the noodles and rinse with cold water. Grab a mixing bowl and combine ricotta cheese, egg, leftover parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit (190 degrees Celsius).
  4. For assembly, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce at the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange six noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with 1/2 of ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3rd of mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups of the meat sauce over mozzarella. Sprinkle that with 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese. Repeat for layers and top the last layer with mozzarella and parmesan. Cover with foil and spray underside of foil with cooking spray to prevent sticking.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes. Let cool for 15 minutes before serving.
